Purpose

The purpose of this website is to inform the viewers about the weather and regional information for certain parts of Idaho. This website is both visually pleasing to view, and easy to navigate. This ensures that the user is able to quickly find the information that is important to them. The weather and regional information greatly impacts our lives, and our goal at www.climatecompound.com is to create a useful website, to view at home on your laptop, and to view on the go on our mobile website. By having regularly updated and accurate weather information, you can better plan your day, and better plan your life. Weather you are visiting, travelling, or just curious, knowing the weather can greatly influence your plans.
