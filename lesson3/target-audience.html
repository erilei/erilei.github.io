Persona 1

Persona: Mom
Fictional Name: Jane Stevenson
Job title: Stay at home mom
Demographics:
  • 29 years old
  • Married
  • 3 kids (7 months old, 5 year old, 2 year old)
  • College Grad, now stay at home mom
Goals and Tasks: A great mother and wife. This mom plans...
  • Walks with her kids and friends
  • vacations with her family
Environment: She is busy with her family. She is on her phone to check her email and social media accounts, knows enough about internet to keep her WiFi working, but loves easy to navigate sites.
Quote: "I love to help my family!"

Scenarios

  • Jane is planning a camping trip with her family in 3 days, and needs to know the weather.
  • Jane needs to know if it will get cold tonight so she knows if she can open her kids' window tonight.
  • Jane needs to know the weather for the morning so she knows if she can plan a walk with her kids when they wake up.
  • Jane needs to know the weather for the next day so she can know if her kids can walk to school.
  • Jane's kids want to go swimming and they need to know the weather so they know if they can play in the sprinklers or not.

Persona 2

Persona: College student
Fictional Name: Kyle Atkinson
Job title: No formal job, just attending school.
Demographics:
  • 22 years old
  • Single
  • 3 older siblings
  • Junior in school
Goals and Tasks: He loves staying physically fit and spending time outdoors going...
  • Hiking
  • Running
  • Going for walks
Environment: Kyle is busy and enjoys hanging out with friends, going on dates, and visiting his family. Kyle is not very tech savvy, so he only uses the internet to complete homework and easy tasks.
Quote: "I have got to get to class on time today!"

Scenarios

  • Kyle needs to know if the weather is going to be good tonight because he wants to go to a bonfire.
  • Kyle is taking a date hiking and wants to know if the weather will be cold or warm.
  • Kyle wants to walk to class, and needs to know if he needs to bring a coat or an umbrella.
  • Kyle wants to know if it is going to snow. If it snows, the school parking lot will be closed.
  • Kyle wants to play sports outside with his friends. He needs to know if he should bring a coat to the field.
